# Refund Policy

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all new subscriptions.
1. Eligibility: Refunds are available to customers who request them within 30 days of their initial purchase.
2. Exclusions: Subscriptions that have consumed more than 75% of their monthly API limits or are on enterprise/custom plans are not eligible for a refund.
3. Process: Customers must request a refund by contacting support or opening a ticket. Once approved, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to the original payment method.
